Ziqi Wang, Xiaoyu Xia, Ibrahim Khalil, Minghui Liwang, and Minhui Xue's paper **[MoSEEC: Sustainable and Trajectory Privacy-Preserving Edge Resource Management](https://doi.org/10.1109/TMC.2025.3635650)** has been published in *IEEE Transactions on Mobile Computing*.

The work appears in *IEEE Transactions on Mobile Computing* 25(5): 6369-6382 (2026), and studies sustainable edge resource management with trajectory privacy preservation.

Congratulations to Ziqi Wang, Xiaoyu Xia, Professor Ibrahim Khalil, Minghui Liwang, and Minhui Xue.
