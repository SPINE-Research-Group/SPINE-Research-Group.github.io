Asitha Kottahachchi Kankanamge Don and Ibrahim Khalil's paper **[Q-VFL: quantum-enhanced vertical federated learning with contrastive encoding for privacy-preserving medical AI](https://doi.org/10.1007/s42484-026-00385-6)** has been published in *Quantum Machine Intelligence*.

The work appears in *Quantum Machine Intelligence* 8(1): 44 (2026), and explores quantum-enhanced vertical federated learning with contrastive encoding for privacy-preserving medical AI.

Congratulations to Asitha Kottahachchi Kankanamge Don and Professor Ibrahim Khalil.
