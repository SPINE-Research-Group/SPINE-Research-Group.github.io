SPINE has received CSIRO iPhD support for the project **Enhancing organisational cybersecurity with AI-driven threat intelligence and Large Language Models (LLM)**.

The project will investigate how AI-driven threat intelligence and LLMs can help organisations improve cybersecurity readiness, analysis, and response.

Congratulations to Chief Investigator Professor Ibrahim Khalil.
