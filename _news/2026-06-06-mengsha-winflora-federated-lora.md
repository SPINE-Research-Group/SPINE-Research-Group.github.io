Mengsha Kou, Xiaoyu Xia, Ziqi Wang, Ibrahim Khalil, Ruikun Luo, Jingwen Zhou, and Minhui Xue's paper **[WinFLoRA: Incentivizing Client-Adaptive Aggregation in Federated LoRA under Privacy Heterogeneity](https://doi.org/10.1145/3774904.3792295)** has been published at *WWW 2026*.

The work appears in *WWW 2026*: 5241-5252, and studies incentives for client-adaptive aggregation in federated LoRA under privacy heterogeneity.

Congratulations to Mengsha Kou, Xiaoyu Xia, Ziqi Wang, Professor Ibrahim Khalil, Ruikun Luo, Jingwen Zhou, and Minhui Xue.
