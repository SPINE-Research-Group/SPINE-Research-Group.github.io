## Job Description: PhD Positions

**Privacy-Aware Intelligent Digital Twin for Secure Critical Infrastructures**  
RMIT University | 3 PhD positions available

This is a draft placeholder job description for the PhD positions on the Privacy-Aware Intelligent Digital Twin for Secure Critical Infrastructures project.

- Conduct research on secure and privacy-aware digital twin systems
- Explore machine learning, encryption, blockchain, and trustworthy AI methods for critical infrastructure
- Develop prototypes, simulations, datasets, and evaluation tools
- Prepare research publications, reports, and presentations

- Strong academic background in computer science, cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, machine learning, data science, engineering, or a related field
- Interest or experience in digital twins, critical infrastructure security, privacy-preserving technologies, or intelligent systems
- Strong programming and analytical skills
- Good communication, writing, and teamwork skills

### Desirable Experience

- Digital twin platforms or cyber-physical systems
- Privacy-preserving machine learning or applied cryptography
- Blockchain, secure data sharing, or trustworthy AI
- Critical infrastructure, IoT, smart grid, transport, or industrial systems
