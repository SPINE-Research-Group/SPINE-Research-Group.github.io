Shehan Edirimannage, Ibrahim Khalil, Xun Yi, and Kwok-Yan Lam's paper **[SecHeto-FL: A Secure and Resilient Federated Learning Framework for Heterogeneous IoT Networks](https://doi.org/10.1109/TNSE.2026.3663098)** has been published in *IEEE Transactions on Network Science and Engineering*.

The work appears in *IEEE Transactions on Network Science and Engineering* 13: 6772-6789 (2026), and proposes a secure and resilient federated learning framework for heterogeneous IoT networks.

Congratulations to Shehan Edirimannage, Professor Ibrahim Khalil, Xun Yi, and Kwok-Yan Lam.
