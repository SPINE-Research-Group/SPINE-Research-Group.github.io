Amani Aldahiri, Ibrahim Khalil, Mohammad Saidur Rahman, and Mohammed Atiquzzaman's paper **[A privacy-preserving class imbalance mitigation framework for face recognition](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.hcc.2025.100325)** has been accepted in *High-Confidence Computing*.

The work appears in *High-Confidence Computing* 6(1): 100325 (2026), and proposes a privacy-preserving framework for addressing class imbalance in face recognition.

Congratulations to Amani Aldahiri, Professor Ibrahim Khalil, Mohammad Saidur Rahman, and Mohammed Atiquzzaman.
