Charitha Elvitigala, Ibrahim Khalil, Shehan Edirimannage, Mohammed Atiquzzaman, and Wathsara Daluwatta's paper **[Intent-Driven Dual-Layer Model Pruning for Energy-Efficient Hierarchical Federated Learning in IoT With Non-IID Data](https://doi.org/10.1109/JIOT.2026.3659554)** has been published in *IEEE Internet of Things Journal*.

The work appears in *IEEE Internet of Things Journal* 13(9): 18415-18428 (2026), and studies energy-efficient hierarchical federated learning for IoT systems with non-IID data.

Congratulations to Charitha Elvitigala, Professor Ibrahim Khalil, Shehan Edirimannage, Mohammed Atiquzzaman, and Wathsara Daluwatta.
