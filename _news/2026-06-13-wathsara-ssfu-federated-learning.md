Wathsara Daluwatta, Ibrahim Khalil, Shehan Edirimannage, Charitha Elvitigala, Jer Shyuan Ng, and Dusit Niyato's paper **[SSFU: Selective Semantic Feature Unlearning for Federated Learning in 6G Internet of Things Systems](https://doi.org/10.1109/JIOT.2025.3625756)** has been published in *IEEE Internet of Things Journal*.

The work appears in *IEEE Internet of Things Journal* 13(1): 852-867 (2026), and studies selective semantic feature unlearning for federated learning in 6G IoT systems.

Congratulations to Wathsara Daluwatta, Professor Ibrahim Khalil, Shehan Edirimannage, Charitha Elvitigala, Jer Shyuan Ng, and Dusit Niyato.
