## Job Description: Postdoctoral Position

This is a draft placeholder job description for the postdoctoral position on the Privacy-Aware Intelligent Digital Twin for Secure Critical Infrastructures project.

### Desirable Experience

- Critical infrastructure security or cyber-physical system modelling

- Trustworthy AI, explainable AI, or resilient intelligent systems
