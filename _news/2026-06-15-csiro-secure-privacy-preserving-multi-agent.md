SPINE has received CSIRO Industry PhD Program support for the project **Secure, privacy-preserving, and trustworthy multi-agent interoperability framework**.

The project will advance trustworthy interoperability for multi-agent systems, with a focus on security, privacy preservation, and dependable collaboration.

Congratulations to Chief Investigator Professor Ibrahim Khalil, Dr Dilum Bandara, and Mr Roshan Elvitigala.
