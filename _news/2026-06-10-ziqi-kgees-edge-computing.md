Ziqi Wang, Xiaoyu Xia, Ibrahim Khalil, Minghui Liwang, Xiaolong Xu, Xun Yi, Yan Li, and Minhui Xue's paper **[KGEES: An Energy Saving System With Location Privacy Preservation in Multi-Access Edge Computing](https://doi.org/10.1109/TDSC.2025.3641177)** has been published in *IEEE Transactions on Dependable and Secure Computing*.

The work appears in *IEEE Transactions on Dependable and Secure Computing* 23(2): 3814-3827 (2026), and studies energy saving with location privacy preservation in multi-access edge computing.

Congratulations to Ziqi Wang, Xiaoyu Xia, Professor Ibrahim Khalil, Minghui Liwang, Xiaolong Xu, Xun Yi, Yan Li, and Minhui Xue.
