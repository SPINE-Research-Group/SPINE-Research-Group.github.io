Exciting news for SPINE: ARC Linkage Project funding has been announced for **LP250200550: Privacy Aware Secure Quantum Machine Learning Platform**, led by **Professor Ibrahim Khalil** at RMIT University.

The project will develop a safe, privacy-focused framework for quantum federated learning, bringing together quantum computing, secure distributed learning, privacy protection, and explainable quantum decision-making.

With announced funding of **$568,955**, the project aims to help Australia adopt quantum AI safely in high-impact areas such as health, finance, and critical services, while supporting the growth of the emerging quantum security sector.

Congratulations to Professor Ibrahim Khalil and the SPINE team on this outstanding announcement.
