## Job Description: PhD Positions

**Federated Fine-Tuning Framework for Secure and Collaborative GenAI Models**  
RMIT University | 2 PhD positions available

This is a draft placeholder job description for the PhD positions on the Federated Fine-Tuning Framework for Secure and Collaborative GenAI Models project.

### Key Responsibilities

- Conduct research on secure and privacy-aware federated fine-tuning for generative AI models
- Develop methods for collaborative training of large language models and multimodal foundation models
- Build prototypes, benchmarks, and evaluation pipelines
- Prepare research publications, reports, and open research tools
- Collaborate with academic researchers and industry partners

### Selection Criteria

- Strong academic background in computer science, artificial intelligence, machine learning, data science, cybersecurity, or a related field
- Interest or experience in generative AI, large language models, federated learning, privacy-preserving AI, or secure distributed systems
- Strong programming skills, preferably in Python and modern machine learning frameworks
- Good analytical, communication, and academic writing skills

### Desirable Experience

- Large language models or multimodal foundation models
- Federated learning, distributed training, or model fine-tuning
- Privacy-preserving machine learning, secure AI, or data protection
- PyTorch, TensorFlow, Hugging Face, or related ML tooling
