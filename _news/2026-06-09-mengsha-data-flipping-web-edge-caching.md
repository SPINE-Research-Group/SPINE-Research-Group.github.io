Mengsha Kou, Xiaoyu Xia, Ibrahim Khalil, Ziqi Wang, Xiuzhen Zhang, Lin Yao, and Minhui Xue's paper **[Data Flipping Attack and Defense in Web Edge Caching Systems](https://doi.org/10.1109/TIFS.2026.3663056)** has been published in *IEEE Transactions on Information Forensics and Security*.

The work appears in *IEEE Transactions on Information Forensics and Security* 21: 2505-2519 (2026), and examines data flipping attacks and defenses in web edge caching systems.

Congratulations to Mengsha Kou, Xiaoyu Xia, Professor Ibrahim Khalil, Ziqi Wang, Xiuzhen Zhang, Lin Yao, and Minhui Xue.
